The Best Movies According to Leyi

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Dune

I like Paul (obviously haha), Timothee is handsome. And Villeneuve incredibly changed such a greate novel into a great movie.

Tenet

The final space-time loop is awe inspiring. Your future is his past. He died in your past, living in your future.

The Ghost in the Shell

Beautiful retro visuals, and the deep reflection on human existence is unforgettable.
